## Personality Descriptors (Cautious Synthesis)

Based on limited peer testimony and self-presentation:

- **Serious, uncompromising**: No evidence of irony or lightness in published work or interviews; Derrida collaboration suggests mutual respect for rigor
- **Modest but determined**: Chose rural philosophical school over prestigious metropolitan university; maintained IRI directorship while running pharmakon.fr
- **Socially conscious**: Political trajectory (PCF → critique of neoliberalism) suggests moral consistency; collective projects (Ars Industrialis, Internation) reflect non-hierarchical values
- **Existentially engaged**: Prison memoir and late political work suggest Stiegler inhabited his philosophy rather than treating it as abstract system

### Caveats on Personality Claims:
- No full-length authorized biography in English available for consultation
- Colleague/student recollections largely inaccessible in English-language sources
- Self-presentation in *Acting Out* and interviews cannot be treated as objective personality report (A+ text content, but subject to authorial framing)

Stiegler emerges from available sources as:
